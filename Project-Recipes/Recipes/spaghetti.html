Spaghetti

Description

The classic Italian dish loved around the world. Homemade spaghetti is the perfect family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 1 can tomato paste 6 oz
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tb spoon brown sugar
  • 16 oz spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger in large pan
  2. Once cooked add salt, pepper, tomato paste, water, brown sugar, and garlic.
  3. Simmer on low for 1 hour.
  4. 10 minutes before the hour is done, cook spaghetti noodles as directed on box.
  5. Once noodles are to your preferred tenderness, strain water and serve.

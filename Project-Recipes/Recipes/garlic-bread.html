Garlic Bread

Description

What would spaghetti be without garlic bread? Still delicious that's what but having a crispy side never hurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Italian bread
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tb spoon of fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix butter, garlic and parsley together in a bowl.
  3. Half bread and cover in butter mix.
  4. Cook in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Add parmesan and serve.

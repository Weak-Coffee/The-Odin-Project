Butter Noodles

Description

Simple. Delicious. Hearty.

Ingredients

  • 2 tb spoons of butter
  • 16 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a rumbling boil.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles.
  3. Once tender, strain noodles
  4. Drown noodles in butter and parmesan cheese.
  5. Eat.
